Frequently Asked Questions

What materials do you use in your jewelry?

We use only the finest materials including 14k and 18k gold, sterling silver, genuine pearls, and ethically sourced diamonds. All materials are carefully selected for quality and durability.

Do you offer custom jewelry design?

Yes, we offer custom design services for special occasions. Please contact our design team through our contact form or visit one of our showrooms to discuss your vision.

How do I determine my ring size?

We provide a comprehensive size guide on our website. You can also visit any of our locations for a professional sizing, or we can send you a free ring sizer tool.

Do you offer jewelry cleaning services?

Yes, we offer complimentary cleaning and inspection services for all J & T Gems And Jewellery pieces. Simply bring your jewelry to any of our locations.

What is your warranty policy?

All our jewelry comes with a one-year warranty covering manufacturing defects. We also offer extended warranty options for an additional fee.

Shipping Information

Free Standard Shipping

We offer free standard shipping on all orders. Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days within the continental United States.

Express Shipping

Express shipping is available for an additional fee and typically delivers within 2-3 business days. Orders placed before 2 PM EST will ship the same day.

International Shipping

We ship internationally to select countries. Shipping times and fees vary by location. Please contact us for specific international shipping information.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can track your package's progress in real-time through our website.

Returns & Exchanges

30-Day Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your jewelry, you can return it within 30 days of purchase for a full refund or exchange.

Return Conditions

  • Items must be in original condition with all tags and packaging
  • Custom or personalized items are not eligible for return
  • Returns must be accompanied by the original receipt or order confirmation
  • Shipping costs for returns are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective

Exchange Process

To exchange an item, please contact our customer service team. We'll guide you through the process and help you find the perfect piece.
